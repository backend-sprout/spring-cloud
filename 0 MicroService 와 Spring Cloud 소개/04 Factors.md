12 Factors
================
> Heroku 개발 회사에서 제시한 12가지 항목 

1. BaseCode
2. Dependency isolation 
3. configurations 
4. linkable backing services 
5. stages of creation 
6. stateless processes
7. prot binding 
8. concurrency 
9. disposability 
10. development & production parity 
11. logs 
12. admin processes for eventual processes
